TIPS AND TRICKS

Learn and improve your gameplay style with these tips and tricks

  1. BE FRIENDLY - do not ruin your game by trashtalking your teammates; we know stuffs happen, their lane is losing, but you will have a better game and higher chance to win if you will not trashtalk your teammates.
  2. MAKE IT  SIMPLE - if you cannot defeat your lane enemy, maybe because of stat gap or his champion counters yours, do not be aggressive that it will cost your lane and your game.
  3. BE AWARE  - every wave clear try to have a glimpse on the minimap. Maybe your jungler needs help because he encountered the enemy jungler; maybe there is a clash going on and you know that your appearance will help your team to win. And place a ward to increase map control.
